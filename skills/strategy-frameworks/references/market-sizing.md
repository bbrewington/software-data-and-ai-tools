# Market Sizing Reference

Detailed methodology for TAM/SAM/SOM analysis.

## Overview

Market sizing estimates the potential revenue opportunity for a product or service. The TAM/SAM/SOM framework provides three levels of increasingly realistic estimates.

## The Three Tiers

| Tier | Definition | Question |
|------|------------|----------|
| **TAM** | Total Addressable Market | How big is the entire market? |
| **SAM** | Serviceable Addressable Market | What can we realistically serve? |
| **SOM** | Serviceable Obtainable Market | What can we realistically capture? |

## Calculation Methods

### Top-Down

Start with industry data and narrow:

```
TAM = Total market value (from reports)
SAM = TAM × % addressable by your model
SOM = SAM × Realistic market share
```

### Bottom-Up

Build from unit economics:

```
SOM = Target customers × Revenue per customer
SAM = SOM ÷ Target market share
TAM = SAM expanded to full market
```

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| TAM = Total economy | Define specific market |
| Only top-down | Validate with bottom-up |
| Ignoring competition | Be realistic about share |
