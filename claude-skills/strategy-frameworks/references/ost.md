# OST (Objectives, Strategies, Tactics) Reference

Detailed methodology for strategic planning using OST.

## Overview

OST (Objectives, Strategies, Tactics) is a hierarchical planning framework that links high-level goals to actionable tasks. It creates clear line-of-sight from purpose to execution.

## The Three Levels

| Level | Question | Time Horizon | Owner |
|-------|----------|--------------|-------|
| **Objectives** | What do we want to achieve? | 1-3 years | Leadership |
| **Strategies** | How will we achieve it? | 6-12 months | Senior team |
| **Tactics** | What specific actions? | Weeks-months | Teams |

## Level Details

### Objectives

- High-level outcomes you want to achieve
- Aspirational but achievable
- Limited in number (3-5 max)
- Stable over planning period
- Answer: "What does success look like?"

### Strategies

- Approaches to achieve objectives
- Choices about where to focus
- Multiple strategies per objective
- Answer: "What's our approach?"

### Tactics

- Specific actions and initiatives
- Measurable and assignable
- Time-bound deliverables
- Answer: "What do we do?"

## Writing Good Elements

### Objectives

| Good | Bad |
|------|-----|
| "Become market leader in SMB segment" | "Grow" |
| "Achieve operational excellence" | "Improve things" |
| "Build world-class engineering team" | "Hire people" |

### Strategies

| Good | Bad |
|------|-----|
| "Focus on enterprise accounts" | "Sell more" |
| "Build self-service onboarding" | "Make it easier" |
| "Partner with system integrators" | "Get help" |

### Tactics

| Good | Bad |
|------|-----|
| "Launch partner portal by Q2" | "Work on partnerships" |
| "Hire 3 senior engineers by March" | "Build team" |
| "Reduce response time to <4 hours" | "Be faster" |

## Alignment Check

### Top-Down

1. Does each objective support the mission?
2. Does each strategy clearly advance an objective?
3. Does each tactic implement a strategy?

### Bottom-Up

1. Is every tactic linked to a strategy?
2. Is every strategy linked to an objective?
3. Are there orphan activities?

## OST vs. OKRs

| Aspect | OST | OKRs |
|--------|-----|------|
| Structure | 3 levels | 2 levels |
| Focus | Actions | Outcomes |
| Metrics | In tactics | Key results |
| Best for | Planning | Goal-setting |
| Cadence | Annual/project | Quarterly |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Problem | Solution |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Too many objectives | Lack of focus | Limit to 3-5 |
| Strategies as tactics | Skipping the "how" | Distinguish levels |
| Tactics without owners | No accountability | Assign every tactic |
| No success criteria | Can't measure progress | Add metrics |
| Static plan | Doesn't adapt | Review regularly |

## Cascading OST

```
Company OST
    ↓
Department OST
    ↓
Team OST
    ↓
Individual OST
```

Each level's tactics become the next level's objectives.
