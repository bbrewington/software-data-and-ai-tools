# Horizon Model Reference

Detailed methodology for the Three Horizons framework.

## Overview

The Three Horizons model helps organizations balance investments across current business, emerging opportunities, and future bets. Developed at McKinsey, it provides a portfolio view of innovation and growth initiatives.

## The Three Horizons

| Horizon | Focus | Time Frame | Risk | Return |
|---------|-------|------------|------|--------|
| **H1** | Core business | 0-12 months | Low | Predictable |
| **H2** | Emerging opportunities | 12-36 months | Medium | Growing |
| **H3** | Future bets | 36+ months | High | Uncertain |

## Horizon Characteristics

### Horizon 1: Extend and Defend

- Current core business
- Existing customers and products
- Incremental improvements
- Operational excellence focus
- Cash generation for other horizons

### Horizon 2: Build Emerging Business

- New capabilities being scaled
- Adjacent markets or products
- Proven but not yet profitable
- Requires investment and focus
- Bridge between today and tomorrow

### Horizon 3: Create Options

- Experiments and explorations
- Disruptive possibilities
- Small bets, big potential
- Research and early pilots
- Seeds for future H2/H1

## Investment Allocation

### Typical Distribution

| Organization Type | H1 | H2 | H3 |
|-------------------|----|----|----|
| Mature/stable | 70% | 20% | 10% |
| Growth-focused | 50% | 30% | 20% |
| Disruption-facing | 40% | 40% | 20% |

### Allocation Considerations

- Industry disruption rate
- Competitive dynamics
- Financial health
- Risk tolerance
- Strategic ambition

## Migration Patterns

### Healthy Portfolio

```
H3 → H2 → H1 (continuous pipeline)
- H3 experiments graduate to H2
- H2 businesses mature into H1
- H1 eventually declines, replaced by new H1
```

### Warning Signs

| Pattern | Problem |
|---------|---------|
| All in H1 | No future growth |
| Empty H2 | Gap between today and tomorrow |
| H3 never graduates | Innovation theater |
| H1 declining | Core business erosion |

## Managing Each Horizon

| Aspect | H1 | H2 | H3 |
|--------|----|----|----|
| Metrics | Revenue, margin | Growth rate, milestones | Learning velocity |
| Leadership | Operators | Entrepreneurs | Explorers |
| Governance | Tight controls | Stage gates | Light touch |
| Funding | Budget cycle | Milestone-based | Small, fast |
| Failure | Unacceptable | Learning | Expected |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Problem | Solution |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Starving H2/H3 | No future | Protect innovation budget |
| H1 metrics on H3 | Kills experiments | Match metrics to horizon |
| No graduation path | Ideas die | Clear stage gates |
| Separate teams entirely | No transfer | Intentional bridges |
| Static horizons | Markets shift | Reassess regularly |
